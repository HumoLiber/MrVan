FAQ

What is MrVan Partners?

+

MrVan Partners is a platform that connects campervan owners and rental companies with travelers looking to rent campervans. We provide the technology, marketing, and customer service infrastructure to help you grow your campervan rental business.

How much does it cost to join?

+

Our pricing varies depending on the plan you choose and the number of vehicles in your fleet. We offer flexible options including commission-based models and subscription plans. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do I need to have my own insurance?

+

Yes, all vehicles listed on our platform must have proper commercial insurance coverage. We can recommend insurance providers that specialize in campervan rentals if needed.

How are payments handled?

+

We handle all payment processing through our secure platform. Customers pay through our system, and we transfer the funds to you after deducting our service fee. Payouts are typically processed within 24-48 hours after a rental is completed.

Can I set my own pricing and availability?

+

Absolutely! You have full control over your pricing, availability calendar, and rental terms. Our system also offers dynamic pricing suggestions based on market demand to help maximize your revenue.

What support do you provide to partners?

+

We provide comprehensive support including a dedicated account manager, technical assistance, marketing materials, and 24/7 customer service for both you and your renters. We also offer training on how to use our platform effectively.
